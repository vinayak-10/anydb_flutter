Privacy Policy for anydb

Effective Date: May 25, 2026

Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how the anydb application handles your data, particularly in connection with your Google Account, Google Sign-In, and Google Drive integrations.

Key Data Guarantee: anydb is a local-first application. We do not run proprietary databases or external servers to collect your personal transaction ledgers. All transaction data is stored locally on your physical device.

1. Information We Collect and Access

Our application interacts with the following services under your explicit consent:

  • Google Account Identity: When you use Google Sign-In, we request access to basic identity info (your email address and profile name) purely to establish your secure user session and cloud configuration.
  • Google Drive Storage (Sensitive Scope): We request the restricted https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file scope. This permission allows our application to create, read, and write only the backup files that were specifically created by the anydb app. We cannot view, access, or modify any other files or folders in your personal Google Drive storage.

2. How We Use Your Data

We utilize the requested Google Drive access solely to provide the cloud backup utility:

  • To export your local encrypted ledger data JSONs into your Google Drive under a structured directory for safety.
  • To fetch and restore your transaction history if you switch devices or perform a manual restore action.

3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Transfer

We do not share, sell, lease, or transfer any user data, credentials, or Google account information to third parties. All data operations occur directly between your physical mobile device and Google's official API endpoints. No intermediary developer servers have access to your data streams.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Since your backup files are stored entirely within your personal Google Drive account, you have complete control over them. You can delete your database backup files directly from your Google Drive folder at any time. To revoke our application's access to your Google Account, you can manage permissions in your Google Account Security Settings.

5. Compliance with Google API Services User Data Policy

anydb's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at your support email address.
